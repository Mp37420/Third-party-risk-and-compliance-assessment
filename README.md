# Third-Party Risk & Compliance Assessment

## 📌 Project Overview
Conducted a third-party cybersecurity risk and compliance assessment for supplier DataGoblyn to evaluate security posture, identify control gaps, and assess overall risk exposure.

---

## 🔍 Key Highlights
- Assessed compliance across 8 security domains (policies, access control, monitoring, incident response)  
- Identified critical gaps in secure SDLC, logging/monitoring, and third-party oversight  
- Evaluated supplier risk posture using likelihood-impact analysis  
- Developed actionable remediation and continuous monitoring plan  

---

## ⚠️ Risk Level
**High Risk** due to lack of proactive security controls, monitoring, and vendor risk management
